Privacy Policy — Nocturnum Tools

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Nocturnum Tools builds browser extensions and digital software tools, including Discogs Digger and LiaPlugin. We keep data handling limited to what is needed to provide, support, and improve our products.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe and, for some browser extensions, ExtensionPay. Payment processors may collect the email address, billing details, and payment information needed to complete a purchase. We do not receive or store full card numbers.

Product data

Individual products may store local preferences or license status needed for the product to work. Product-specific privacy details are provided on each product page when applicable.

Support

If you contact us for support, we use the information you provide only to answer the request and resolve the issue.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers needed to operate payments, licensing, support, and security, or when required by law.

Contact
